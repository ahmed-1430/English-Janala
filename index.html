Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! Our core website and all its essential features are completely free to use. We are committed to providing valuable resources and tools at no cost to our users.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, an account is not required. You are welcome to browse and use the vast majority of our website's content completely anonymously and without signing up.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Building a strong vocabulary is a journey, and the most effective strategies involve consistent, engaging exposure to the language. The key is to find methods you enjoy and will stick with.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes! We offer a digital Certificate of Completion for many of our courses to help you showcase your new skills and knowledge.
